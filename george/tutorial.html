⚖️ Our rules

🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Add up to 10 players by entering their names.

▶ Hit "Start Game" to begin the fun!

❓ Hypothetical questions will appear — players answer privately.

🕵‍♂ After each round, players guess who gave each answer.

🎭 Chat, bluff, and deceive your friends — each player can edit their answer once.

✅ Press "Which is Who" to reveal the answers, earn points, and celebrate the winner!

🧠 Scoring System...

✅ Correct Guess = +10 Points

🧮 Max Score = 10 x Number of Players

🏆 Winner = Player with the highest score

💾 Logged in? Your score will be saved to your account!
